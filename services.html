Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

Do you repair all brands of washing machines?

Yes, our certified technicians are trained to repair all major brands and models, including commercial industrial washers and domestic machines.

What is included in the Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)?

Our AMC includes routine inspections, cleaning, lubrication of moving parts, calibration, and priority emergency support to ensure zero downtime.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency breakdown?

For commercial clients, we offer a rapid response time of 2-4 hours depending on your location within our service area.

Do you use genuine spare parts?

Absolutely. We only source and use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) genuine spare parts to guarantee the longevity and safety of your machines.
